Banaue Rice Terraces

Banaue Rice Terraces in Ifugao is the world's oldest rice terraces. The 2,000-year-old terraces were carved by indigenous Filipinos' ancestors. The terraces are 1,500 meters above sea level and have an ancient but efficient irrigation system.
